Community

Our Story

COMPASS was created to help high school students, especially recent immigrants, navigate the U.S. school system and prepare for college. The program provides guidance, support, and a community where students can ask questions and explore their future.

Our Mission

To help students confidently navigate high school, prepare for college, and discover their academic and career paths.
